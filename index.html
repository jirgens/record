About

Big Love

Big Love records is a very small independent record label located in Ann Arbor Michigan. We have Big Love for every form of music, and or artistic endeavor. We believe that everyone has a voice and everyone deserves a platform from which to be heard. We would love to hear from you and are always interested in new talent and support on any level. If you're interested in getting involved with Big Love please contact us. We would love to hear from you.
